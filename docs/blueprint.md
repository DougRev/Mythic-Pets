# **App Name**: Mythic Pets

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure user login with Google, Apple, or Email/Password. Includes free tier and pro plan tracking.
- Pet Profile Creation: Create and manage profiles for multiple pets, including name, breed, age, species, bio, reference images and color palette.
- AI Persona Generation: Transform pet photos into AI-generated art based on user-selected themes (Superhero, Detective, Knight, Wizard, Cyberpunk, etc.) and image styles, accompanied by a short lore text (~100-150 words). User has ability to regenerate.
- AI Story Generation: Generate a story based on the selected persona, with options for tone (Wholesome, Funny, Epic, Mystery, Sad) and length (Short Post, Story Page, Full Tale). Includes pet name, persona info and optional consistent lore elements.  The AI tool outputs a short title and a story with the option to regenerate, edit, or save.
- Story Gallery: Display all generated stories in a gallery with thumbnails, titles, dates, and a 'Share' button.  Allows users to favorite stories or download them as image or PDF.
- Content Sharing: Enable easy sharing of pet personas and stories as images with preformatted social captions and hashtags, along with a 'Remix this for your pet' CTA.

- Background color: Light blue (#D4E2FC), offering a calming and visually unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Purple (#A020F0), used to highlight interactive elements, provide a magical feel and create contrast.
